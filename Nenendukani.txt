   Nee Sannidhilo Nee Pondu Kori – Nee Snehithudanaithine

   2. Nee Shramalalo – Paalondutaye – Naa Darshanamaayene
   Naa Thanuvanduna – Shramalu Sahinchi – Nee Vaarasudanaithine
   Aha! Naa Dhanyatha Oho! Naa Bhagyamu – Emani Vivarinthunu || Nenendukani ||

   3. Neelo Nenundute – Naalo Neevundute – Naa Athmeeya Anubhavame
   Parishuddhaathmuni Abhishekamutho – Ne Paripoornatha Chendeda
   Aha! Naa Dhanyatha Oho! Naa Bhagyamu – Emani Vivarinthunu || Nenendukani ||
