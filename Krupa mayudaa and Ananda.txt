A Small Correction for Sanskrit
In your list, you wrote "He Satya eshvara". To be grammatically and spiritually accurate in Sanskrit, it is better to say:

* He Satya Parameshvara (adding Parama makes it "Supreme," which matches the weight of "Allah").
