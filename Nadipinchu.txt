"Namo Avalokiteshvara" is a Buddhist chant 
honoring the Bodhisattva of Compassion, often
used in Mahayana traditions.

"Ya Allah" is an Arabic invocation meaning 
“O God,” used in Islam to call upon Allah, the one God.

"Oh the Only true God" is a general monotheistic phrase, 
often used in Christian, Jewish, or Islamic contexts to
affirm belief in one supreme deity.
